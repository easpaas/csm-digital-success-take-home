<h2 className="font-bold text-xl">What is Vercel?</h2>
<br/>

Vercel is a platform designed to help teams build and deploy high-performance websites and applications quickly. Vercel handles the complexities of deployment, caching, and performance monitoring, allowing your marketing team to focus on creating engaging content without worrying about the technical details. Whether you're managing a blog, landing pages, or e-commerce site, Vercel ensures your website is fast, reliable, and easy to maintain.
